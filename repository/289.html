    Honor Cord/Diploma Information

    Honor Cord

    A gold cord worn around the neck of the gown signifies baccalaureate graduation with academic honors. Cal Poly Pomona grants honors upon graduation to students who have demonstrated academic excellence throughout their undergraduate careers.

    Graduates receiving master's/postbaccalaureate degrees are not eligible for honors.

    Honors apply to undergraduate students who graduate in Fall or Spring terms only, with an overall grade point average of at least 3.50. Spring Semester candidates must have had a minimum overall GPA of 3.50 based on Fall grades. Everyone who earns honors at graduation will have that designation noted on their diploma.  The honors are:

    • Summa Cum Laude: 3.80 - 4.00
    • Magna Cum Laude: 3.65 - 3.79
    • Cum Laude: 3.50 - 3.64

    Distribution Dates at Student Service Building 121 - Breezeway

    • Monday - March 21 - 9:30 a.m. to 1:30 p.m.
    • Wednesday - March 23 - 9:30 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.

    Additional dates will be announced by end of March.  For information or questions, please email commencement@cpp.edu.

    Diploma Information

    Diplomas are sent to the students' mailing addresses on file. Those to whom a degree has been awarded will receive a diploma email from the Registrar's office asking that they verify the accuracy of their mailing address. This notice will be sent to the Cal Poly Pomona email account.

    The Senior Exit Survey is attached to the diploma email.  The diploma will be mailed out once the survey is submitted.

    To change your address on-line, please visit https://www.cpp.edu/broncodirect

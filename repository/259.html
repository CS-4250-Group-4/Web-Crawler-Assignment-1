    Beyond the 6: A Resume Wokeshop

    The Latino Professional Alumni Chapter & the Cesar E. Chavez Center are partnering up to provide a resume workshop to students and alumni! Six seconds is the average time a recruiter spends reviewing an applicant’s resume - eliminating 75% of applicants on this first step. The Beyond the 6 WOKESHOP reframes and decolonizes the resume writing process; from how to highlight your cultural capital and lived expertise, to how to demonstrate the impact and skills you bring to the table.

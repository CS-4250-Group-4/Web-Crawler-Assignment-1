    Quarantine and Isolation Process

    If you have tested positive for COVID-19 and/or a health care provider thinks you have COVID-19:

    Regardless of vaccination status, previous infection or lack of symptoms, Los Angeles County Department of Public Health requires that you isolate.  

    If you are a close contact to someone who has COVID-19, you must follow the Los Angeles County Department of Public Health quarantine requirements. 

    Do not come to campus until you have been cleared by the university’s Safer Return Incident Investigation Team.

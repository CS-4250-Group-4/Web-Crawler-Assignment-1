    What to Bring to Orientation Day

    Bring the following with you to your in-person Orientation Day:

    • Laptop or tablet, with charger, to use during advising and registration (if you have one)
    • Printed copy of your parking permit (Instructions how to get your parking permit will be in your reminder emails)
    • Completed CPP Release of Liability and Visual/Audio Image Release Form (Your parent or guardian must sign if you will be under 18 on your in-person Orientation Day)
    • A copy of your unofficial transcripts and/or test scores, in case you need to reference them during advising and registration
    • Comfortable shoes
    • Reusable water bottle
    • Sunscreen and hat
    • Backpack, notebook, and pen
    • Light sweater or jacket for the morning, evening, and indoors

    The average summer temperatures in Pomona range from the high 80s to the mid-90s (Fahrenheit). There will be portions of Orientation Day that are outdoors so you should come prepared.

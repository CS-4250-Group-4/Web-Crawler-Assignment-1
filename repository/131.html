    Transportation services is dedicated to meeting your mobility needs. Please email rideshare@cpp.edu for questions regarding transportation needs.

    Safer Return Guidelines

    • Face covering is required to ride the Bronco Express Shuttle
    • Each shuttle is equipped with signage and equipment to ensure social distancing
    • Each shuttle will undergo frequent sanitation processes to ensure a safe space

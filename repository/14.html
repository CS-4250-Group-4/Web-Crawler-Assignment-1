    Summer 2022

    Summer 2022 Orientation reservations will be available from March 15 to May 9. Summer orientation is for students starting in Fall semester.

    We are planning for an in-person, on-campus orientation for all incoming students. In-person events will be held in line with all local health and safety guidelines. Check the official Cal Poly Pomona Safer Return website for current information.

    Information will be updated on our website throughout the month of March. Please check back often!

    Orientation is the beginning of your Cal Poly Pomona experience and ensures a successful transition into the academic community. Orientation is designed for incoming freshmen and transfer students, in addition to parents and family members.

    Freshman Orientation

    Summer 2022

    Summer 2022 Orientation reservations will be available from March 15 to May 9. Summer Orientation is for students starting in the fall semester.

    We are planning for an in-person, on-campus orientation for all incoming students. In-person events will be held in line with all local health and safety guidelines. Check the official Cal Poly Pomona Safer Return website for current information.

    Orientation is a mandatory enrollment requirement for all new students. Pay close attention to all orientation details and deadlines as failure to complete your orientation requirement will result in your admission being withdrawn.

    More information will be updated on our website throughout the month of March. Please check back often!

    Orientation Components

    1. Orientation Reservation

    REQUIRED

    Make your Orientation reservation beginning March 15. The reservation deadline is May 9 at 11:59 p.m. Pacific Time. You will select your in-person, on-campus orientation date when making your reservation.

    More Information about Making Your Reservation

    2. CPP Online Orientation

    REQUIRED

    The CPP Online Orientation will be available beginning May 15. You must complete the first two parts of CPP Online Orientation before your Orientation day in order for your Orientation Registration hold to be removed.

    Go to the CPP Online Orientation (Beginning May 15)

    3. Orientation Day

    REQUIRED

    Attend your reserved Orientation day, which will be in-person and on-campus at CPP! This is the day you meet your fellow new students, get connected with campus resources, and register for your first-semester classes!

    More Information about Orientation Day

    Bronco Family Orientation

    You may add up to two guests to your Orientation reservation for a fee of $65 per guest. Your guests attend the in-person Bronco Family Orientation on the same day as your student orientation. They will also receive access to the Bronco Family Online Orientation, be subscribed to the Bronco Family e-newsletter, and receive access to a variety of resources. Guests can be anyone in your support network who is supporting you during your time at CPP, such as parents, grandparents, spouses, older siblings, or family members. Please note the Bronco Family Orientation is geared toward adult family members or supporters and is not recommended for young children or siblings.

    Spanish translation services will be available on June 10 and July 1.

    More Information about the Bronco Family Orientation

    Why Do I Need to Participate in Orientation?

    • Orientation prepares you for academic success by introducing you to faculty and staff, teaching you about registration tools and academic culture, and connecting you to important academic support resources.
    • Orientation helps you connect to the campus community by providing opportunities to meet other students.
    • Orientation empowers you to be an active learner by teaching you about CPP policies, introducing you to resources to help when times are tough, and giving you the tools you need to become a critical thinker.

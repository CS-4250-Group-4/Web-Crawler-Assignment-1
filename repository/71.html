      • Blue Shield of California HMO - Covid-19 Resources for Blue Shield members:
        • Virtual Care-Teladoc: Members can speak with a board-certified physician for $0 until end of May where they can check their symptoms through phone or video chat anytime and anywhere in the United States. Any medications prescribed during the visit will be sent to the member’s nearest pharmacy. Members can access this benefit through the Blue Shield of California mobile application or 1(800)835-2362.
        • 24/7 Nurse Advice Line: Get health advice 24 hours a day, 7 days a week from a registered nurse to get answers to any health-related questions. Services are available online through their Blue Shield login, or our toll-free telephone number (877)304-0504.

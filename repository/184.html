    Current Canvas Status

    • Canvas is currently fully configured and available to all faculty and students.
    • Staff can request Canvas accounts by emailing canvas@cpp.edu.
    • Canvas is the ONLY LMS available to use with students. Faculty will be able to access content in Blackboard through at least Spring 2022. If students require Blackboard access to resolve incomplete courses, their faculty should email canvas@cpp.edu.
    • We have 24/7 "Tier 1" support for Canvas from now through June 30 , 2022. See the Support page for details. Note that Canvas support can help only with Canvas-specific issues, not with Cal Poly Pomona systems.

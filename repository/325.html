    Contact Us

    Have a question about Orientation at CPP? Don't hesitate to reach out! You can reach us via our website chat, the contact form below, email, or phone. When sending an email or leaving a voicemail, include your full name, 9-digit Bronco ID number, specific question, and contact information.

    If you have difficulty or are unable to use the Orientation chat service, please call (909) 869-3604 or email orientation@cpp.edu for assistance, Monday to Friday, 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m., Pacific Time.

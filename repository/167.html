    All of US at CPP

    Cal Poly Pomona is one of five campuses across the nation chosen to bring the All of Us program to our students. By participating in this unique opportunity, Cal Poly Pomona students will contribute to a national database that is poised to eliminate health disparities and reflect the diversity of our country. This rich data source will help advance prevention and find treatment for all people.

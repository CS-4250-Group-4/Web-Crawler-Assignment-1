    Families and Supporters

    Welcome to the Bronco Family

    Family members and supporters play a critical role in student success at Cal Poly Pomona, and we want to make sure you have the information you need to support your student. The Bronco Family Orientation Experience provides a comprehensive introduction to CPP resources, programs, and support services. For more details please visit our FAQ page.

    Summer 2022

    Bronco Family Summer Orientation 2022 will be conducted both virtually and in-person aligning with the continued health policies and regulations as outlined on the safer return website.

    Bronco Family Orientation Registration

    Students can add up to two guests when they make their orientation reservation through their student portal. If you need assistance with registering parents, family members and/or supporters contact our office at (909) 869-3604, or via email at orientation@cpp.edu. Guests who would like to speak to someone in Spanish can contact our office at (909) 869-6988.

    Bronco Family Orientation Experience Components

    • Online Orientation (Available starting on May 15)
    • In-person, on-campus Orientation Day (dates vary starting on June 8)
    • Bronco Family e-newsletter subscription

Student Services

Cal Poly Pomona University is a special place filled with committed staff and faculty who are here to help you succeed.  We believe that the best way to learn is by doing; here you'll collaborate with faculty and other students to apply knowledge to real-world problems. We foster a supportive and vibrant campus environment where you’ll find ample opportunities to explore your interests and pursue your dreams.  As we are a wonderfully diverse community of scholars; here you'll meet, learn from and make friends with faculty, staff and students whose varied experiences and perspectives will challenge your thinking and prepare you to thrive in an increasingly diverse and interdependent world.

Your possibilities here are boundless, limited only by the choices you make and by the strength of your determination to succeed. All of us in Student Services are here to help you make the most of your Cal Poly Pomona experience. Through the Career Center, Student Heath and Counseling Services, and Student Health Services, we offer resources and services to help you clarify your values, define and achieve your career goals, as well as maintain good health and wellness. Through the Disability Resource Center, and Student Support and Equity Programs, we offer comprehensive programs and services for students with disabilities, former foster youth, students overcoming educational and economic disadvantage, and undeclared students.

Please contact our departments if you have questions or if you'd like additional information. We also welcome and appreciate your comments and suggestions.

Thank you for enriching our campus community by your presence!

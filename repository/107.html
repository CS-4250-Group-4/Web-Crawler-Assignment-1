    Student Conduct and Integrity

    The Office of Student Conduct and Integrity is working in in-person and ocassionally working remotely per the President’s directive until further notice.

    Our office is here to assist students, faculty, staff and the campus community in keeping a safe and fair environment both inside and outside of the classroom. Staff are available and will respond to email and voicemails within one business day.

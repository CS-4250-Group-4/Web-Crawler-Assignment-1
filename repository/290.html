    Graduation Requirements

    Graduation refers to the term in which you have officially and successfully completed all of your graduation requirements. You must apply for the term in which you plan to graduate through your Student Center in BroncoDirect.  Once grades are posted, your record will be evaluated and if all requirements are met, your degree will be awarded.

    Applying for Spring 2022 Graduation

    Apply for Spring 2022 graduation if you are in senior standing and going to complete all your requirements to graduate at the end of Spring Semester; or

    Apply for Summer 2022 graduation if you are in senior standing and going to complete all your requirements to graduate at the end of Summer 2022.

    Undergraduate and Graduate students expecting to graduate Spring or Summer 2022 need to apply online via BroncoDirect for graduation by March 18, 2022, to participate in ceremonies, and have name displayed on the Class of 2022 grad list.

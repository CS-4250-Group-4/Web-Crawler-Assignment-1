    Making Your Orientation Reservation

    Before You Make a Reservation

    • Before you can make your reservation, you must submit your Intent to Enroll and pay your Enrollment Deposit (if applicable). If you received an enrollment deposit waiver, you must still submit your Intent to Enroll.

    Making Your Reservation

    • Make your Orientation reservation through BroncoDirect. You must make your reservation by 11:59 p.m. Pacific Time on May 9.
    • If you do not make your reservation by the deadline, your admission to CPP will be withdrawn.
    • Making your Orientation reservation signs you up for the CPP Online Orientation and the in-person, on-campus Orientation Day you select.
    • Reservations are first-come, first-served. Dates will close when they reach capacity so we recommend making your reservation as soon as possible. Not all academic departments are available on every date. On BroncoDirect, you will see only the dates with space available for your admitted academic major.
    • To make your reservation:
      • Log in to MyCPP.
      • Click on Reserve Your Spot in Orientation.
      • Follow the prompts to confirm your reservation.
      • You can also log in to BroncoDirect from MyCPP and then navigate to New Students, then Make Your Orientation Reservation.
    • You will receive a confirmation email when your reservation is confirmed.

    Adding Guests ($65 per guest fee)

    • You may add up to two guests to your Orientation reservation for a $65 per person fee when you make your Orientation reservation. Guest space is limited and first-come, first-served.
    • Your guests will attend their in-person, on-campus Bronco Family Orientation on the same day as your student orientation.
    • The Bronco Family Orientation runs concurrent to the student orientation with separate sessions tailored to the needs of parents, family members and other supporters.
    • Spanish translation services will be available on June 10 and August 1. If your guests would utilize these services, we recommend selecting one of these dates if your major allows.
    • In addition to the in-person orientation, your guests will receive access to the Bronco Family Online Orientation, be subscribed to the Bronco Family e-newsletter, and receive access to a variety of resources throughout the year.
    • Guests can be anyone in your support network who is supporting you during your time at CPP, such as parents, grandparents, spouses, older siblings, or family members.
    • The Bronco Family Orientation is geared toward adult family members or supporters and is not recommended for younger siblings.
    • If you did not add guests when you made your reservation and want to add guests at a later date, contact our office to inquire about guest availability. Unfortunately you cannot make changes online after you confirm your reservation.

    Changing Your Date

    • You cannot change your Orientation Day online after confirming your reservation.
    • If you need to change your Orientation Day, contact the Office of New Student Programs & Family Engagement as soon as possible before the start of your Orientation Day. If you do not attend your scheduled Orientation Day, your admission to CPP will be withdrawn.
    • Your admitted academic major must have space available on your requested date in order for the change to be processed. 
    • You are only able to change your date once.

    CPP Online Orientation

    • The CPP Online Orientation is a self-paced online course, divided into three parts:
      • Part 1 will be available on May 15.
      • Part 2 will be available on June 1.
      • Part 3 will be available on August 1.
    • You must complete the first two parts of the CPP Online Orientation for your Orientation Registration (ROS) hold to be removed. Your ROS hold will be removed one to two business days after you finish Parts 1 and 2 of CPP Online Orientation.
    • You must complete the third part of the CPP Online Orientation by August 25, 2022.
    • Completing the CPP Online Orientation means viewing all sections and passing the final test with a score of 80% or higher. You will receive a certificate via email once you successfully complete the entire CPP Online Orientation.
    • If you do not complete the entire CPP Online Orientation, you will have a registration hold for your next semester class registration.

    Orientation Day

    • Your Orientation Day is when you come to campus and have the opportunity to meet new students, current students, staff, and faculty; learn about your college; receive academic advising and register for your first-semester of CPP classes!
    • You select your Orientation Day when you make your Orientation reservation. You only attend one Orientation day.
    • Orientations Days are held in-person and on-campus in accordance with campus health and safety guidelines. Please review your reminder emails carefully for important information about health and safety protocols that you will need to follow to keep Orientation safe for everyone!
    • Orientation is a full day program, so plan to be at CPP for the entire day.
    • If you do not attend Orientation Day, your admission to CPP will be withdrawn. If you can no longer attend your reserved Orientation Day, contact us AS SOON AS POSSIBLE to reschedule. Be sure to include your Bronco ID so we can assist you quickly.
